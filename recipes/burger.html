Air Fried Cheeseburgers

Description

Cheeseburgers using an air fryer!

This is a simple recipe to get that fast food fix.
This recipe uses frozen patties. No thawing required!

Ingredients

  • 2 Frozen Hamburger Patties
  • 2 Pepper Jack Cheese Slices (or Your Preferred Cheese)
  • 2 Hamburger Buns
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Optional: Line your Air Fryer Basket with Aluminum Foil
  2. Use Non-stick Spray or Olive Oil on Your Air Fryer Basket
  3. Lightly Coat Your Frozen Patties with Non-stick Spray or Oil
    • This will allow the seasoning to stick.
  4. Add Patties to Basket
  5. Season the Patties with Salt and Pepper (along with any additional seasonings)
  6. Set Air Fryer to 380F and Timer to 15 Minutes
  7. Flip Patties After 7-8 Minutes
  8. At 45-60 Seconds Left, Add Cheese Slices to Patties
  9. Place Cheese Patties on Plate
  10. Place Buns Side Down onto the Basket's Cooking Juices
  11. Cook Buns for 45 seconds
  12. Place Patty into Buns, add Toppings and/or Sauces
  13. Enjoy!
