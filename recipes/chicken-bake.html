Chicken Thighs Baked

Description

Chicken Bake! This is a simple and delicious dish to create.

Chicken Bake is a dish that is entirely dependent on the seasoning.
Feel free to add any additional spices or your own personal marinade.
This particular recipe will be using bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs.

Ingredients

  • Bone-in, Skin-on Chicken Thighs
  • Baking Sheet
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Oregano
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Add 1 Tbsp Each of Salt, Pepper, Chilli Flakes, and Oregano to a Bowl
  2. Pat Down 4 Chicken Thighs with Paper Towels
  3. In a Large Bowl, Add the Chicken Thighs and a Tbsp of Olive Oil
  4. Mix in Seasoning then transfer to Baking Sheet
  5. Heat Oven to 425 degrees F
  6. Cook for 35 Minutes
  7. Pull Chicken Thighs Out and Rest for 10 Minutes
  8. Serve with Rice or Anything and Enjoy!
